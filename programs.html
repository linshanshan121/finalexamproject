  • Preschool programs (15 months-5 years)

    Buttercups is an interactive activity and curriculum kit for children ages 0-36 months, which develop gross and find motor skills, as well as provide music, language, art, cognitive,social, and sensory experience. The daily lesson plans include more activities to choose from and provide suggestions for different ages and abilities.

    Registration Fee (annual)
    $30 per child Deposit ($138-$174 – based on age) enroll, parents must pay the registration fee PLUS one-week’s tuition (both are NON-REFUNDABLE if your child does not attend. The tuition can be used toward your first week if your child starts here.)
    Group Weekly Rates
    15 months to Two years $174.00, Two to Three years $151.00, Three to Five years $138.00

    NOTE: The following fees are due EACH week regardless of attendance or center closings. You are paying for your child a spot (like a membership), NOT the days your child actually attends!

  • School age programs (5-12 years)

    Do you need Before and/or After School care for your 5 to 12-year-old child? If so, we have just the place! Check out our cost-effective, fun before and after school programs. We offer an After School program at Bostian Elementary and a Before/After School program at China Grove Elementary. For our main location at South Rowan Academy, we accept DSS-subsidy for your convenience.
    Before/After School programs (5-12 years)
    The After School program offered at South Rowan Academy is for subsidized children only. To qualify, parents must contact the Department of Social Services at 704-216-8330. We are only offering ONE before/after school program off site in the China Grove Elementary gym.
    Registration Fee (annual)
    $30 per child To enroll, parents must pay the registration fee PLUS the first month’s tuition (both are NON-REFUNDABLE if your child does not attend).
    Rate (2 days/week) during Covid-19/Virtual Learning)
    Before School $25/month, After School $50/month, Before & After School $75/month

  • Summer Camp (5-11 years) 2021 Rates

    South Rowan Academy of Child Development believes that extracurricular activities develop children in a myriad of ways. We see to it that for rising kindergartners up to 11 years old (elementary school only), they will be continually learning outside the classroom setting for them to become better individuals in the near future. At the two locations of our summer camp program, we conduct field trips 3-5 times a week which include bowling, skating, and swimming! We offer both full-time and part-time care.

    Registration
    $30/child (one time fee, if not already enrolled in after school)
    Full-time Rate
    $154 per week per rising kindergartner and $140 per week per school-age child (ages 6-11) ($10 discount on each additional child enrolled in full-time).
    INCLUDES field trip and transportation costs!NOTE
    Full-time Tuition is due weekly for a minimum of 8 weeks (excludes one-week tuition-free vacation). Full-time is NOT based on attendance; you are paying for a spot for the entire summer camp.
    Rate
    $160/week per child (no discount given) or $630/month with $10 discount. Includes field trips and transportation costs!

    NOTE: Due to our space limitations, right now, we are unable to offer care at a part-time rate. If you only need part-time care, we can keep your child on our waitlist and let you know if we have space available. If approved, parents must call us and let us know when their child is coming each week.
